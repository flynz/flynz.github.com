    新西兰坎特伯雷紧缺职业列表 CSSL - FLYabroad

    新西兰坎特伯雷紧缺职业列表 CSSL ( Canterbury Skill Shortage List ) 适用于 基础技能类工作 Essential Skills instructions 或 工作转居留 Work to Residence 类临时签证的申请。CSSL 列表中的职业是坎特伯雷地区大地震之后重建所需的职业。

    职业组 Occupational Group - FLYabroad 职业代码 ANZSCO - FLYabroad 对持有雇主offer的工作签证申请人的要求 Required standard for work visa applicants with an offer of employment - FLYabroad

    National Diploma in Building Control Surveying, Building, Construction Management or Architectural Design/Technology OR Bachelor Degree in Building Surveying or Building Science AND two years experience
    Construction Construction Project Manager (Chip sealing, Asphalt or other Technical Manager) (Roading and Infrastructure) (133111) 工程学高等教育文凭以及人事管理培训，并且有10年相关的实践经验。
    Tertiary qualified or Diploma of Engineering and trained in Staff Management AND ten years’ relevant practical experience

    At least five years’ experience in building or maintaining roads AND Staff management experience AND appropriate drivers licence

    Bachelor or Diploma of Engineering or equivalent and Project Management qualification AND a minimum of five years’ relevant experience AND staff management experience

    Bachelor of Construction (Quantity Surveying) AND three years’ relevant post qualification experience

    National Diploma in Civil Engineering (Level 6) or Level 6 Diploma in Civil Engineering and at least three years work experience including at least 12 months relevant work experience in the past 18 months
    Engineering Civil Engineer (233211) 华盛顿协议认可的工程学学位； 或 下列之一被NZQA认可为等同于工程学学士或工程学荣誉学士或工程学硕士学位；或 学士学位加IPENZ关于学位符合新西兰职业工程师注册资格的证明信； 或 新西兰职业注册工程师
    A Washington Accord accredited engineering degree OR Either a qualification specifically recognised by NZQA as comparable to a: • Bachelor of Engineering, or • Bachelor of Engineering (with Honours), or • Master of Engineering degree OR A bachelor degree (Level 7) or higher with a letter from IPENZ certifying that the degree and any further learning meets the academic requirements for registration as a ‘Chartered Professional Engineer’ in New Zealand OR NZ registration as a ‘Chartered Professional Engineer’
    Engineering Electrical Engineer (233311) 华盛顿协议认可的工程学学位； 或 下列之一被NZQA认可为等同于工程学学士或工程学荣誉学士或工程学硕士学位；或 学士学位加IPENZ关于学位符合新西兰职业工程师注册资格的证明信； 或 新西兰职业注册工程师
    A Washington Accord accredited engineering degree OR Either a qualification specifically recognised by NZQA as comparable to a: • Bachelor of Engineering, or • Bachelor of Engineering (with Honours), or • Master of Engineering degree OR A bachelor degree (Level 7) or higher with a letter from IPENZ certifying that the degree and any further learning meets the academic requirements for registration as a ‘Chartered Professional Engineer’ in New Zealand OR NZ registration as a ‘Chartered Professional Engineer’

    Bachelor Degree (Level 7) qualification majoring in computer science, information science or information technology AND three years of relevant work experience

    注意： Notes:
    Note 1: 学历需要与列出的新西兰学历相当 Qualifications must be comparable to the standard of the New Zealand qualification listed.
    Note 2: 如果职位要求有工作经验，那么无论什么学历都需要满足 Where work experience is included as a requirement it applies to all qualifications listed for the occupation (unless otherwise indicated).
    Note 3: 当括号中标注：“此领域紧缺学历为：...”时，申请人如果只是为了满足职业要求，无需持有紧缺学历。Where a specification lists a qualification in a bracket stating "a qualification(s) in this area of absolute skill shortage is/are", a person does not need to hold that qualification in order to meet the requirement of the list.
    Note 4: 如果级别相同，那么就认为“新西兰证书” 或 “新西兰文凭”的与国际证书或文凭等同。A "New Zealand Certificate" or "New Zealand Diploma" is deemed to be equivalent to a particular National Certificate or Diploma if the levels stated are the same.
